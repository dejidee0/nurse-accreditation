Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about NNEC services, registration, and examinations

Registration Questions

How do I register as a new nursing professional?

To register as a new nursing professional:

  1. Visit our registration page
  2. Select your user type (Fresh Applicant - Trained in Nigeria/Abroad)
  3. Fill in your personal information accurately
  4. Upload required documents (ID, certificates, passport photo)
  5. Submit your application and await verification
  6. Complete payment once approved
  7. Receive your index number via email

The entire process typically takes 3-5 business days after document submission.

What documents do I need for registration?

Required documents include:

  • Valid ID: National ID, Driver's License, or International Passport
  • Nursing Certificate: Original certificate from your nursing school
  • Academic Transcript: Official transcript from your institution
  • Passport Photograph: Recent passport-sized photo
  • Birth Certificate: For age verification
  • Additional documents: May be required based on your training location

All documents must be clear, readable, and in English (or officially translated).

Can I register if I trained abroad?

Yes! We welcome internationally trained nurses. The process includes:

  • Select "Fresh Applicant - Trained Abroad" during registration
  • Provide credential evaluation from recognized agencies
  • Submit translated documents (if not in English)
  • Complete additional verification steps
  • May require bridging courses depending on your training

Processing time for international applicants is typically 7-10 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Debit/Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, Verve
  • Bank Transfer: Direct transfer to our designated accounts
  • Mobile Money: All major mobile payment platforms
  • Online Banking: Internet banking from all major banks
  • USSD Payments: Quick dial codes for instant payment

All payments are processed securely with bank-level encryption.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my application?

Refund policy varies by stage:

  • Before Processing: 100% refund (minus processing fee)
  • During Review: 75% refund available
  • After Approval: 50% refund (administrative costs apply)
  • After Examination: No refund available

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days with valid reasons.

What should I expect during the examination?

The NNEC examination consists of:

  • Duration: 3 hours (180 minutes)
  • Format: Multiple choice and scenario-based questions
  • Sections: Clinical Practice, Ethics, Patient Safety, Legal Issues
  • Passing Score: 70% minimum
  • Results: Available within 48 hours

Bring valid ID, examination slip, and writing materials. Electronic devices are not permitted.

What happens if I fail the examination?

If you don't pass on your first attempt:

  • You'll receive detailed feedback on areas for improvement
  • Retake opportunities are available in the next examination cycle
  • Additional study materials and resources will be provided
  • One-on-one counseling sessions are available
  • Retake fee applies (₦10,000)

Most candidates pass on their second attempt with proper preparation.

Certificate Questions

How do I download my certificate?

To download your certificate:

  1. Log into your dashboard
  2. Navigate to the "Certificates" section
  3. Click "Download" next to your certificate
  4. Enter your verification PIN (sent via SMS/email)
  5. Download the PDF certificate

Certificates are available for download within 24 hours of passing your examination.

How can employers verify my certificate?

Certificate verification options:

  • Online Verification: Visit our verification portal
  • QR Code: Scan the QR code on your certificate
  • Certificate Number: Enter your unique certificate number
  • Phone Verification: Call +234 800 NNEC (6632)
  • Email Verification: Send request to verify@nnec.ng

All certificates include security features to prevent forgery.

Technical Questions

I'm having trouble uploading documents. What should I do?

Common solutions for upload issues:

  • File Size: Ensure files are under 5MB each
  • File Format: Use JPG, PNG, or PDF formats only
  • Internet Connection: Check your internet stability
  • Browser: Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Clear Cache: Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Disable Extensions: Temporarily disable browser extensions

If issues persist, contact our technical support team.

Can I access my account from multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your NNEC account from multiple devices:

  • Desktop computers and laptops
  • Tablets and mobile phones
  • Any device with internet access
  • Automatic synchronization across devices
  • Secure login with two-factor authentication

Always log out from public or shared devices for security.
