How to Register for Indexing

Follow these simple steps to complete your professional registration

1

Create Profile and Login

Enter required information, verify your email address, and access your dashboard.

2

Apply for Indexing

Fill the application form. School officer validates your information. If cleared, proceed to payment. If rejected, receive edit notification.

3

Make Payment

Select your preferred payment gateway and complete the transaction securely.

4

Receive Index Number

Index number is issued after approval and payment confirmation. Disapproved users will be notified with reasons.

Next Step: Apply for Examination

Once indexed, you can proceed to apply for professional examinations.
